About CrackSmith

CrackSmith is a powerful terminal-based tool designed to efficiently crack hashes. It offers robust capabilities like hash detection, resume modes, live statistics, system monitoring, and webhook integration for notifications.

How It Works

CrackSmith automates the process of cracking hashes using various techniques such as dictionary attacks, brute-force, and hybrid methods. Simply load your hash files, choose your wordlist and attack mode, and let CrackSmith do the rest. Get notified through Discord webhooks upon completion or if hashes are cracked.
